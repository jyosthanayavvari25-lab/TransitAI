TransitAI System Architecture

TransitAI is built to streamline urban transport accessibility for senior citizens, visually impaired commuters, students, and tourists. By removing the dependency on legacy bus timetables and routing directories, TransitAI optimizes commutes in high-stress, unfamiliar environments.

Neural Routing Engine

Uses Gemini-3.5-flash with custom schema models. Grounding queries analyze weather logs, traffic levels, and historical delay matrices to compute fastest multi-modal pathways.

IoT Telemetry Caches

Connected with dummy IoT units mapping satellite coordinate streams. Every 4 seconds, bus speeds, fuel ranges, driver details, and cabin crowd indexes are updated recursively.

Adaptive Accessibility

Incorporate Web Speech synthesis and recognition capabilities. Visually impaired commuters can utilize screen readers, speak destinations, or scan stop stickers instantly.
